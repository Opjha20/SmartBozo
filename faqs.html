Your Questions Answered

1. What is SmartBozo?

SmartBozo is a platform that delivers bite-sized lessons on complex topics directly to your WhatsApp. Learn smarter, not harder!

2. How does it work?

Subscribe to any course, and you'll receive daily learning snippets via WhatsApp. Each snippet is designed to simplify complex concepts.

3. How much does it cost?

SmartBozo offers a freemium model. You can try some courses for free, while advanced content is available via paid subscriptions starting at ₹99/month.

4. Can I subscribe to multiple courses?

Yes! You can subscribe to multiple courses and receive snippets for each of them daily.

5. How do I start learning?

Simply choose a course from our Courses Page, sign up, and start learning on WhatsApp.
